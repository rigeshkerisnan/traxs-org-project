About Company

Trax Solutions Sdn Bhd (TRAX) is a local bumiputra company, established since 2007, to focus on the supply of medical equipment. As a medical equipment supplier company, TRAX have been appointed by the principal, Siemens Malaysia to provide views and advisory services to users on the latest systems provided and appropriate with the hospital’s workflow. TRAX has been fully engaged in providing equipment supplies and consultation for government and private hospitals in their procurement. Our main strength is the teamwork as well as always prioritizing the client’s needs. Based on the company’s experience and the trust of the principal, TRAX has successfully secured more than RM70 million contracts since 2007. In the past few years, TRAX has branched into trading, project management, consultation and provision of manpower, working hand-in-hand with some manufacturers and system integrators in the Information and Communication Technology, and Transportation (railway-related, locomotive and rolling stocks) sectors. In year 2021, TRAX is working in collaboration with Metro Transit Solution Pte. Ltd. From Singapore in providing management consultancy services to the Mass Rapid Transit and Light Rail Transit Systems in Malaysia. In year 2022, TRAX has formed a Joint Venture collaboration with Binasat Communications Berhad in providing services to upgrade Huawei’s 4G network capacity. TRAX intends to expand the company’s potential by taking opportunities in the electric vehicle industry. Our assessment towards the potential of the business for the past few years has seen the growth of the industry. By taking part in the industry, TRAX has opportunities to contribute in the development of the green technology for a better nation and environment in the future.
